METHODOLOGY

This section includes the materials and methods used in the study.

Materials

The researchers procured 1 L of distilled water which will act as the mixture solvent. The researchers also procured 250 mL of dishwashing liquid which will act as the mixture solute. Beakers and a stirring rod were also used in the study.

Methods

The researchers used the Completely Randomized Design as the main design of the study.
